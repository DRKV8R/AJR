Growth Plan

9-Month Action Plan: The Roadmap to $50k/Month

A granular, three-phase plan to build a powerful foundation and scale the channel's revenue and reach, setting the stage for the $1M/month goal.

Months 1-3

Phase 1: Foundation & Audience Growth

Establish the content engine, grow the email list, and prepare for initial product launch.

Key Milestones:

  • Content Cadence: Finalize branding and lock in a 2-videos-per-week schedule.
  • Community Building: Launch "Feedback Fridays" live streams to grow a core audience and hit 50k subscribers.
  • Pre-Launch Prep: Develop the first digital product (Sample Pack) and grow email list to 5,000.

Months 4-6

Phase 2: Monetization & Expansion

Launch the first product, integrate affiliate streams, and achieve the first $10k revenue month.

Key Milestones:

  • Product Launch: Execute launch for the first sample pack, targeting $5,000 in initial sales.
  • Affiliate Integration: Publish cornerstone "My Gear" videos and fully integrate affiliate links across all content.
  • Systemization: Hit 100k subscribers, achieve a consistent $10k/month revenue, and hire a freelance editor.

Months 7-9

Phase 3: High-Value Scaling

Launch a high-ticket course, secure the first major brand sponsorship, and scale to $50k/month.

Key Milestones:

  • High-Ticket Course: Develop and launch the flagship "Sound Design Masterclass" with a pre-order campaign.
  • Brand Partnerships: Create a professional media kit, pitch to 10 brands, and secure the first $5,000+ sponsorship.
  • Scaling Revenue: Reach 250k subscribers and achieve the benchmark of $50,000 in total monthly revenue.
